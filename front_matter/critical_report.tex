\eesCriticalReport{
  – & –   & –    & The autograph manuscript of this work (H-Bn Ms.mus II.48,
                   RISM~ID 530001730, dated 1802-01-27) is a setting of the
                   psalm \textit{Dixit Dominus} in four movements.
                   By contrast, \B1 contains five vesper movements.
                   Most likely, the original psalm setting was arranged to a
                   vesper by Johann Wittmann of the Lambach abbey
                   (copy A-LA 1397, RISM~ID 603002682, dated 1802–1823).
                   This is corroborated by the title page of a copy in the
                   Kremsmünster abbey (A-KR E 14/61, RISM~ID 600176395,
                   dated 1823; title page: “[…] Ex Michaelis Haydn unico
                   Psalmo: | Dixit Dominus traductae, et transformatae |
                   per D. Joannem Wittmann, Musicum Lamba- | censem”).
                   The copy in the Domarchiv Salzburg (A-Sd A 561,
                   RISM~ID 659000641, dated 1831) contains an additional
                   xsetting of \textit{Laudate Dominum} by Luigi Gatti. \\
